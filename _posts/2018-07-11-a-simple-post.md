  One of Jekyll’s best aspects is that it is blog aware. What does this
  mean, exactly? Well, simply put, it means that blogging is baked into
  Jekyll’s functionality. If you write articles and publish them online, you
  can publish and maintain a blog simply by managing a folder of text-files
  on your computer.
